Nagaland is a mountains state in Northern India. It is the country of festivals and there was very rich
country and the capital of Nagaland is koshima the Nagaland is a very beautiful place bordering
Myanmar. It's home to diverse indigenous tribes, with festivals and markets celebrating the different
tribes culture. Its capital city of Kohima suffered heavy fighting in World War Il commemorated by
memorials at the Kohima War Cemetery The Nagaland State Museum exhibits ancient weaponry
a ceremonial drum and other traditional Naga cultural artifacts.

Nagaland became the 16th state of India on 1 December 1963. It has experienced insurgency and inter-ethnic conflict since the 1950s, limiting its economic development. 181191 Agriculture is its most important economic activity, accounting for over 70% of its economy. Other significant activities include forestry, tourism, insurance, real estate, and miscellaneous cottage industries. It lies between the parallels of 98 and 96 degrees east longitude and 26.6 and 27.4 degrees latitude north. It is home to a rich variety of flora and fauna. Nagaland is governed by a Council of Ministers, headed by a chief minister, which is responsible to the 60-member Legislative Assembly (Vidhan Sabha). The constitutional head of state is the governor appointed by the president of India. The state is divided into seven administrative districts.
